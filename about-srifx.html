Sustainable Impact Fund

Invest in a way that seeks to build wealth & a better world

The Sustainable Impact Fund (SRIFX) applies two selection screens to find diversified stock funds that have strong recent returns and strong environmental, social and governance (ESG) ratings.

Rigorous Performance Criteria

The first screen uses FundX’s time-tested Upgrading strategy to own core diversified stock funds with strong recent returns.

FundX developed the Upgrading strategy and has used this time-tested approach to manage private client accounts since 1969.

Effective ESG Integration

The second screen integrates environmental, social and governance (ESG) factors into FundX’s Upgrading approach.

This non-financial performance indicator is applied as a risk measure in an effort to generate long-term competitive investment performance and positive societal impact.

Emphasis On Impact Investing

SRIFX seeks to reward funds that actively engage with companies for improvements on environmental, social, or governance issues.

Impact-oriented funds meet our rigorous performance requirements to be included in the portfolio.
