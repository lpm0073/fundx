Rigorous Performance Criteria

Step one uses FundX’s time-tested Upgrading strategy to identify core diversified stock funds that have strong recent returns.

Funds that have done well recently may continue to do well in the coming months or even years, a phenomenon known as ‘persistence of performance’.

Effective ESG Integration

Step two integrates environmental, social and governance (ESG) ratings into FundX’s Upgrading approach.

ESG ratings are a non-financial indicator that we use as a risk measure to try to generate long-term competitive investment performance and positive societal impact.

Emphasis On Impact Investing

Step three considers impact-investing funds. SRIFX can invest more in funds that actively engage with companies to help them improve.

Impact funds must also meet our rigorous performance requirements to be included in SRIFX’s portfolio.
